HTML5 Head Generator

A markup generator for URL display optimizations in social content silos.

The generator supports Twitter cards, Google's structured data and Facebook's Open Graph.

Add this markup to your web pages to make links to your site look great in social apps and websites! Also, making outbound content look attractive.

USAGE: Fill out the form, and then copy and paste the markup into your web page.
